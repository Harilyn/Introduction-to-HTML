Ranking Movies I've seen this year

In no particular order, below is a list of my three favourite Netflix Movies for this year.

The Residence

Money Heist

I rewatched money Heist recently, and I was still impressed by the complex characters and intricate plot twists. The way the story unfolds, with each character's motivations and backstories slowly being revealed, is a masterclass in storytelling. The combination of action, suspense, and witty dialogue makes for a thrilling ride, and the chemistry between the cast members is undeniable.

Seven Deadly Sins

Seven Deadly Sins captivated me with its powerful blend of action, emotion, and fantasy. The story of Meliodas and his band of misfit knights is not just about cool fights and magic—it’s also about how much the characters care about each other, even when things get really hard. Each person in the group has their own special story, which makes the show even more interesting. What truly drew me in was the emotional connection between the characters and how their personal struggles shaped the choices they made. Seven Deadly Sins isn’t just thrilling—it’s fun, but it also has a lot of heart.
